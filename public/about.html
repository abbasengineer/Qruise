About Cruise Control Optimization App

The Cruise Control Optimization App is designed to help drivers find the best segments of their route for using cruise control. By analyzing road gradients and traffic conditions, the app provides recommendations on where to use cruise control for a smoother and more efficient driving experience.

Our goal is to enhance driving safety and comfort by providing actionable insights based on real-time data.

Feel free to contribute to our project on GitHub.
